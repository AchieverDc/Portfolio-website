Magister Operis

Role: Developer

Magister Operis is a user-friendly platform designed to connect people with skilled home service providers. Whether you need a Plumber, Electrician, Painter, Web services, and even a makeup artist, Magister Operis makes it easy to find and hire professionals quickly. Users can search for services, read reviews, and get cost estimates for their projects. Providers can also register on the app to offer their services and expand their customer base.
